# Product Definition: SBJoshi Personal Homepage v2

## 1. Vision & Goals
The goal of this project is to create a modern, high-impact personal homepage for a researcher and engineer. The new version will shift the focus towards a professional developer/engineer profile while maintaining the academic depth of the original Hugo-based site.

## 2. Target Audience
*   **Primary:** Industry/Tech Recruiters looking for engineering and research expertise.
*   **Secondary:** Academic peers, potential collaborators, and current/prospective students.

## 3. Core Features & Sections
*   **Professional Summary:** A high-level overview of engineering skills, research areas, and career highlights.
*   **Teaching & Mentorship:** Detailed lists of courses taught, educational materials, and a section on teaching philosophy.
*   **Publications & Research:** A comprehensive, categorized library of published work, including links to PDFs, abstracts, and citations.
*   **Student Supervision:** A dedicated space for current and former students, highlighting their projects and achievements.
*   **Blog & Project Portfolio:** A technical blog for sharing insights and a portfolio to showcase specific engineering projects.

## 4. Visual Identity & User Experience
*   **Style:** Modern Professional Profile – Clean, sophisticated, and polished.
*   **Look & Feel:** High-quality typography, multi-column layouts where appropriate, and a focus on readability and professionalism.
*   **Platform:** Optimized for GitHub Pages using a supported Static Site Generator (SSG) for easy content management.

## 5. Migration Strategy
*   **Approach:** Curated Migration – Content from the existing Hugo site will be reviewed and selectively migrated. This ensures that the new site remains focused and high-quality, reflecting the "v2" vision rather than a direct copy of all historical data.
