# Product Guidelines: SBJoshi Personal Homepage v2

## 1. Content & Tone
*   **Prose Style:** Concise & Technical. Information should be presented clearly and efficiently, allowing for quick scanning by recruiters and technical peers.
*   **Quality Standard:** High Signal, Low Noise. Avoid filler content and prioritize quality over quantity. Every piece of content should serve a clear purpose.
*   **Voice:** Professional, expert, and direct.

## 2. Branding & Visual Design
*   **Brand Image:** Sophisticated & Professional. The site should project a sense of high-end design, expert knowledge, and meticulous attention to detail.
*   **Typography:** Sophisticated serif or high-quality sans-serif fonts, with balanced spacing and clear hierarchy.
*   **Layout:** Clean, organized, and focused on showcasing content effectively without unnecessary distractions.

## 3. User Experience (UX) & Technical Standards
*   **Mobile-First Design:** The site must be fully responsive and provide an excellent experience across all screen sizes, from mobile phones to large desktop monitors.
*   **Performance-Centric:** Pages should load exceptionally fast. Assets (images, fonts, scripts) must be optimized for speed and efficiency.
*   **Accessible & Inclusive:** The site must adhere to modern accessibility standards (e.g., WCAG) to ensure it is usable by everyone, including those using assistive technologies.
*   **GitHub Pages Support:** The site MUST be fully compatible with GitHub Pages for automated hosting and deployment.

## 4. Maintenance & Updates
*   **Simplicity:** Maintenance must be extremely simple. Adding, removing, or modifying content should be achievable primarily through simple Markdown edits or by placing static files (e.g., PDFs, images) in appropriate folders.
*   **Workflow:** Robust and version-controlled. Content changes should be managed through Git, with automated deployments triggered on push to the repository.
*   **Ease of Use:** The underlying system should be easy for a user to understand and manage without requiring complex technical setups for routine content updates.
